# Edge Up Sports — Press Coverage

The main press wave hit in **August 2015** around the IBM Watson partnership announcement and Kickstarter launch. Edge Up Sports was founded in Kansas City by CEO Ilya Tabakh, building a fantasy football analytics platform powered by Watson's cognitive computing and NLP.

---

## Major Publications

- **[WIRED — "IBM's Watson AI Wants to Coach Your Fantasy Football Team"](https://www.wired.com/2015/08/ibms-watson-ai-wants-coach-fantasy-football-team/)** · Aug 12, 2015  
  The splashy launch piece. Described Edge Up as an "AI-powered Rotten Tomatoes for fantasy football" using Watson's entity extraction, sentiment analysis, and Personality Insights APIs.

- **[WIRED — "All the Apps You Should Be Using to Rule Fantasy Football"](https://www.wired.com/2015/09/apps-using-rule-fantasy-football/)** · Sep 12, 2015  
  Follow-up roundup piece: "You're pretty smart, but Watson is smarter."

- **[Quartz — "There's finally a great use for IBM's Watson: making fantasy football picks"](https://qz.com/477323/theres-finally-a-great-use-for-ibms-watson-making-fantasy-football-picks)** · Aug 12, 2015  
  Detailed breakdown of the "cognitive news radar," Watson sentiment analysis on both players and pundits, and the Kickstarter structure.

- **[NBC News — "IBM's Watson Could Help You Win at Fantasy Football"](https://www.nbcnews.com/tech/mobile/ibms-watson-could-help-you-win-fantasy-football-n408496)** · Aug 12, 2015  
  Broad audience tech coverage of the Watson-powered roster decision tools.

- **[Fast Company — "IBM's Watson Can Now Help You Kick Ass In Fantasy Football"](https://www.fastcompany.com/3049711/ibms-watson-can-now-help-you-kick-ass-in-fantasy-football)** · Aug 12, 2015  
  Featured Edge Up as an example of Watson's consumer and entertainment applications.

- **[CIO Magazine — "IBM Watson brings cognitive computing to fantasy football"](https://www.cio.com/article/244823/ibm-watson-bring-cognitive-computing-to-fantasy-football.html)** · Aug 13, 2015  
  IT-focused coverage with extended Ilya Tabakh quotes and fantasy sports market sizing.

- **[eWeek — "IBM's Watson Takes on Fantasy Football"](https://www.eweek.com/database/ibm-s-watson-takes-on-fantasy-football/)** · Aug 2015  
  Enterprise tech angle, with analyst commentary from Charles King of Pund-IT calling it "a great venue for demonstrating Watson's value."

## Key Facts

- **Kickstarter goal:** $35,000 — funded within days, exceeded with a $65k stretch goal
- **Pricing:** $22/season (stat tier) or $55/season (cognitive/Watson tier), $550 for a whole league
- **Funding:** $25k Digital Sandbox KC grant + $30k additional (SEC filing)
- The product was consistently described as a "cognitive news radar" and supplemental tool for Yahoo/CBS fantasy platforms
