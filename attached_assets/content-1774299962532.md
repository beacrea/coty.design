\# Coty Beasley

\> Coty Beasley is a product architect and co-founder of Underline, where he leads product strategy, AI innovation, and design engineering across community infrastructure systems. Based in Manhattan, Kansas, his career spans creative technology, civic product leadership, and infrastructure innovation — from building AI products with IBM Watson in 2015 to leading the first privately funded open-access fiber network in the United States. His work is unified by a commitment to democratizing access to critical systems and making technology genuinely useful for communities.
